# V Forge Set vs. V Prime Set — which should I buy?

Both sets are V Series. Both include a 5-sensor Stick. Both include WiFi cloud monitoring. The difference is **how they measure ambient (cook environment) temperature** and which kind of cook you do most.

## The short version

* **V Prime Set:** one-piece simplicity — meat temperature *and* ambient temperature both come from a single Stick. Best for everyday grilling, oven cooking, sous vide, and any cook where pit-temperature accuracy isn't the top concern.
* **V Forge Set:** two pieces — the Stick measures meat temperature, a separate Wired Ambient Probe measures pit temperature at grate level. Best for long smoker cooks (8+ hours), kamados, and anyone who's been burned by inaccurate pit readings on a wireless probe.

## Side-by-side

| | V Prime Set | V Forge Set |
|---|---|---|
| Stick | V Prime Stick (5 internal + 1 built-in ambient sensor) | V Core Stick (5 internal sensors, no built-in ambient) |
| Ambient temperature measured at | The Stick itself (built-in sensor) | The grate (separate Wired Ambient Probe on a 1 m cable) |
| Pieces in the box | 2 (Stick + Smart Base) | 3 (Stick + WiFi Hub + Wired Ambient Probe) |
| WiFi cloud monitoring | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| Setup pieces to manage | Fewer | One more piece to clip at the grate |
| Best for | Weeknight grilling, sears, oven roasts, sous vide | Long smoker cooks, kamado, low-and-slow BBQ |

## Why the V Forge separates the two measurements

When the ambient sensor sits on the same Stick that's buried in cold meat, the meat acts as a heat sink and pulls the ambient reading well below what's actually happening in the pit. For a quick weeknight cook that doesn't matter much. For a 12-hour brisket where you're trying to hold 225°F overnight, a falsely-low ambient reading can cost you the cook.

The V Forge separates the two so each measurement is accurate, regardless of what's happening to the other one. The trade-off is one more piece to set up.

## A simple rule

* You mostly grill, sear, sous vide, and roast → **V Prime Set**
* You mostly smoke, low-and-slow, kamado, or pellet-grill long cooks → **V Forge Set**
* You do both → **V Forge** is the more capable setup; you can use it for everyday cooks too without clipping the ambient probe.

## What about V Duo or V Core?

* **V Duo Set** — two Sticks + an on-base LCD screen. For multi-protein cooks where you want phone-optional monitoring.
* **V Core Set** — single Stick, no WiFi, lowest entry price. The cheapest way into the V Series ecosystem.
