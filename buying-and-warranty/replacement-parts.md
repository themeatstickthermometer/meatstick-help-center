# Replacement parts

Lost a charger? Bent a cable? Want a spare Stick? Most pieces of your MeatStick setup can be replaced individually — you don't have to re-buy the whole set.

## What you can replace

| Part | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Stick** | A replacement V Series Stick (V Prime or V Core) pairs to your existing app and base. |
| **Base or charger** | V Smart Base, V Duo Smart Base, V Charge Stack, or V WiFi Hub. |
| **Wired Ambient Probe** | The grate-level probe that ships with the V Forge Set. |
| **USB-C charging cable** | Standard USB-C — any quality cable works in a pinch. |
| **Color ID rings** | Yellow / blue / red silicone rings for telling Sticks apart. |

## How to get a replacement

The fastest way is to **[fill out a ticket in the app](https://tms.fyi/help)**. Tell us:

1. Which part you need
2. Which set you have (V Forge Set, V Prime Set, V Duo Set, V Core Set, or a legacy MeatStick)
3. Your order number, if you have it

We'll confirm what's available and the current pricing.

## Expanding vs. replacing

If you're not replacing a broken part but **adding** monitoring points, the [V Core Set](../v-core/set-up.md) is the way to grow — a complete Stick plus a stackable charger. See [Adding more Sticks](../v-forge/adding-more-sticks.md).
