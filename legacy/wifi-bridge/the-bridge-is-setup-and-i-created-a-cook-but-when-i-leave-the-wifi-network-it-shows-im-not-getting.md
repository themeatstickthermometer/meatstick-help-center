# The Bridge is set up and I created a cook but when I leave the WiFi network I'm not getting a signal

If you're encountering signal loss for your cooks outside your WiFi network, here's how you can ensure seamless connectivity:

#### MeatStick Cloud Setup

To receive signals through the cloud, set up MeatStick Cloud:

1. **Create/Log In to Your MeatStick Account:**
   * Log in or create an account within the app.
   * Recipes will be linked to your account.
2. **Account-Linked Recipes:**
   * Recipes created without an account won't provide signals outside your WiFi network.
   * Ensure you're logged in when creating recipes.

#### Troubleshooting Steps for Signal Loss

1. **Delete Non-Delivering Recipe:**
   * Remove the recipe that's not providing signals.
2. **Recreate Recipe:**
   * Log in to your account.
   * Recreate the recipe while connected to the same WiFi as the Bridge.
   * The Bridge will sync and transmit data to the cloud for future reference.

By adhering to these guidelines, you'll ensure consistent connectivity and access to your recipes even outside your WiFi network.
