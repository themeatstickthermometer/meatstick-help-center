# How do I turn off the LCD backlight?

The LCD has two modes for its backlight: an "always-on" mode and a "temporary 30 seconds" mode. To switch between these modes, follow these steps:

#### Always-On Backlight Mode

1. **Long Press:** Press and hold the button.
2. **Release:** Let go of the button.

#### Temporary 30 Seconds Backlight Mode

1. **Long Press:** Press and hold the button.
2. **Release:** Let go of the button.
3. **Activate:** If the backlight is off, press the button to turn it on for 30 seconds.
