# My WiFi Bridge is stuck on booting, what can I do?

Encountering difficulties with your WiFi Bridge stuck in the booting process? Here's how to troubleshoot and resolve the situation:

#### Alternate USB Cable and Power Source

* **Try a Different USB Cable:** Swap the current USB cable for a different one.
* **Switch Power Sources:** Use an alternate power source to ensure sufficient voltage.

If you have any further questions or need additional assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team at [https://tms.fyi/help](https://tms.fyi/help).
