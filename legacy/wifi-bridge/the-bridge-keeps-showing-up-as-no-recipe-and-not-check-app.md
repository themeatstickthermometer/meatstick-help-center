# The Bridge keeps showing up as "No recipe" and not "Check app"?

If your Bridge displays "No Recipe" instead of "Check App," there's no need to be concerned. This status means:

* **Successful Connection:** Your Bridge is connected to your WiFi network.
* **No Re-setup Needed:** The Bridge does not require re-setup.

Now, it's time to get cooking! Create a new cook and enjoy the convenience of your Bridge-enabled MeatStick experience.
