# The MeatStick WiFi Bridge Features

The MeatStick WiFi Bridge offers unique features and unlimited range:

* **Xtender Mode:** If there's no internet, the Bridge acts as an Xtender, connecting via Bluetooth.
* **Internet Connection:** With internet, it syncs to your home's WiFi and sends temperatures to your phone, even if you're away.
