# How long does the Stick need to be charged?

If a Stick's battery is at empty, it will take around 2 hours to fully charge through quick charging depending on the Stick.&#x20;

The charger has LED indicator when quick charging, you can wait for the LED to turn off as that will indicate the Stick is fully charged.
