# Can I use rechargeable batteries?

You can use rechargeable batteries with your MeatStick charger:

* **Black Charger:** Compatible with any type of AA batteries.
* **Orange Charger:** Compatible with any type of AAA batteries.

This flexibility allows for a sustainable and eco-friendly approach to powering your MeatStick charger.
