# How does the Xtender (Red) work?

The Xtender boosts your signal range effortlessly by capturing and amplifying signals from nearby Sticks.

#### Activation

1. **Insert Batteries:**
   * Insert 2 x AA batteries.
2. **Toggle Power:**
   * Toggle the power switch.

#### Usage

* **Positioning:**
  * Place the Xtender close to your Stick for an improved signal.
