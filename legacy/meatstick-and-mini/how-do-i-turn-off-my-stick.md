# How do I turn off my Stick?

The MeatStick and MeatStick Mini automatically turn off after 30 minutes of inactivity or minimal temperature changes. These features are designed to conserve battery life and ensure safety during and after cooking.

Note: The MeatStick Mini lacks an ambient temperature sensor, but this does not affect the auto turn-off function.
