# Can I charge the 2020 Stick with the charger featuring a micro-USB charger (MeatStick 2018 Charger)?

**IMPORTANT!**

The Micro USB-powered charger can only charge the included Stick. It can not charge any Stick from the Battery powered chargers. Doing so will damage the Stick.&#x20;
