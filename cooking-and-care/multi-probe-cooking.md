# Cooking with more than one Stick

Running a brisket and ribs at once? Mains and sides in the oven? You can monitor several Sticks on a single cook, all in the MeatStick App.

## How many at once

The MeatStick App monitors **up to 8 Sticks** at the same time. Activate each Stick one at a time on its charger, and they'll all appear in the app, each managed independently.

The number a single base or hub relays depends on the hardware:

* **V Duo Smart Base** holds and charges 2 Sticks and shows both on its LCD.
* **V WiFi Hub** (Forge) manages the full Forge bundle — up to 4 Sticks.
* Beyond those, you can add more Sticks paired directly to your phone, up to the app's limit of 8.

## Telling your Sticks apart — color rings

When more than one Stick is in the cook, the **colored silicone ID rings** keep them straight. Every V Core Set ships with three (yellow, blue, red).

1. Slip a ring onto each Stick's handle.
2. During pairing in the app, assign that Stick the matching color.
3. The reading you see in the app now matches the ring on the Stick — no more guessing which one is in the brisket and which is in the ribs.

## Adding more Sticks to your setup

The simplest way to add a monitoring point is a [V Core Set](../v-core/set-up.md) — a complete Stick plus a stackable charger. It pairs to the same MeatStick App account and works alongside the Sticks you already have. You never re-buy the smart base or hub.

For the full expansion story — how the chargers stack, how many probes per hub — see [Adding more Sticks to your V Forge](../v-forge/adding-more-sticks.md).

## One ambient reading is enough

If your Sticks include an ambient sensor (the V Prime Stick) plus non-ambient Sticks (the V Core Stick), you only need **one** ambient reading per cook — the cooking environment is the same for every protein in the same smoker or oven. That's exactly why the [V Duo Set](../v-duo/whats-in-the-box.md) pairs one ambient-equipped Stick with one that focuses on internal temperature.
