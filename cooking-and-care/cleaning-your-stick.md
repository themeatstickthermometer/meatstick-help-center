# How should I clean my Stick?

Ensure your Stick remains free from any residue after cooking. Follow these steps for effective cleaning:

1. Use a sponge, soap, and warm running water for a thorough clean. A good scrubbing is usually sufficient to restore its cleanliness.
2. If residue persists on the Stick's round metal cap, you can employ iron wool. However, avoid scrubbing the ceramic parts with the wool.

We recommend that you clean the Stick to the most adequate standard, see below:

**Important !!:** \
Do not soak your Stick in water! This helps maintain its functionality and longevity.

<figure><img src="https://480337956-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-Mg4Jg9fo_8jMW0As7z7%2Fuploads%2F159AoQ5uJDYZacCVj1PD%2F4b363366-3d56-4a49-9c8a-9ffb31b8508b-IMG_0407.webp?alt=media&#x26;token=363ac582-878c-4129-985f-4a87c94e06ca" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>Clean Stick in running water and DO NOT soak.</p></figcaption></figure>

**Additional Note:** \
Ensure the metal springs on the charger are spotless. They serve as crucial contact points between the Stick and the charger.

Never place a wet Stick on the Charger. Charging + Water will damage the charging prongs and cause it to stop charging over time.
