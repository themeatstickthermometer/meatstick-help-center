# Battery and charging

Every MeatStick V Series Stick charges the same way — drop it onto its base or charger and it tops up automatically. The details below apply to **any** V Series Stick (V Prime or V Core). How each *base or charger* behaves is a little different, so there's a quick guide to yours at the bottom.

## Charging your Stick

* **Drop it on to charge.** Place the Stick onto your base or charger. The contacts seat magnetically and charging starts on its own. Leave it about an hour before first use.
* **Quick charge in a pinch:**
  * 5 minutes → about 4 hours of cooking
  * 10 minutes → about 9 hours of cooking
* **Full battery lasts 50+ hours** — long enough for the longest overnight smoke.
* **Don't charge a wet Stick.** Dry it off first so the contacts make a clean connection.
* **Clean before charging.** Wipe the metal cap and steel shaft so they look like bare steel, not soot — residue can block charging.

> **Brand-new Stick looks dead? That's normal.** Sticks ship from the factory in a low-voltage protection state. If you lift a brand-new Stick off the charger before its first charge, it can look like nothing's happening. Give it about 10 seconds on a powered charger and the protection state clears — it'll wake up when you take it off.

## Will my older Stick charge on a V Series base?

No. A V Series base or charger is built for the V Series Stick and has different dimensions. A MeatStick 4, Chef, or older Stick won't seat correctly. Always charge each Stick on the base or charger made for it.

## Your base or charger works a little differently

Charging times and battery life above are the same for every V Series Stick. But how you power your base, what its lights mean, and how long *it* lasts depend on which one you have. Open yours:

* [V Smart Base](../v-prime/smart-base.md) — the base in the V Prime Set
* [V Duo Smart Base](../v-duo/duo-base.md) — the two-Stick base with the LCD screen
* [V Charge Stack](../v-core/charge-stack.md) — the stackable charger in the V Core Set
* [V WiFi Hub](../v-forge/wifi-hub.md) — the hub in the V Forge Set
