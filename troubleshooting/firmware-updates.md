# How do I update the firmware?

We're committed to enhancing accuracy, signal strength, and battery life through ongoing algorithm improvements. When we've thoroughly tested and stabilized new firmware, you can update your Stick to benefit from these enhancements.

Here's what you need to pay attention to when updating the firmware:

**Notification Indication:**\
If new firmware is available for update, a red notification bubble will appear on the menu icon. _**Make sure you're logged into your App account to view this notification.**_

**Accessing the Firmware Update:**\
Open the menu to access the firmware update tutorial for your Stick. Simply click on the notification to initiate the tutorial.

**Time Duration:**\
Firmware updates require a few minutes of your attention. Make sure you're not pressed for time before proceeding with an update. Avoid updating if you're about to begin cooking.

Your active participation in updating the firmware ensures that your Stick remains optimized for top-notch performance.&#x20;

If you have any further questions or need additional assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team at [https://tms.fyi/help](https://tms.fyi/help).
