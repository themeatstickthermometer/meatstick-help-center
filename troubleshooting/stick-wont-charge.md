# My Stick won't charge

Charging problems almost always come down to dirty contacts, a wet Stick, or an unpowered base. Run through these in order.

1. **Clean the contacts.** Wipe the round metal cap and the steel shaft until they look like bare steel, not soot. Residue is the #1 cause of failed charging.
2. **Make sure the Stick is dry.** Never put a wet Stick on the charger — dry it off so the contacts connect.
3. **Confirm the base or charger has power.** Check the USB-C cable is seated and the outlet is live. Your base or charger should show a charging light — see your device's page for what its LED means: [V Smart Base](../v-prime/smart-base.md) · [V Duo Smart Base](../v-duo/duo-base.md) · [V Charge Stack](../v-core/charge-stack.md) · [V WiFi Hub](../v-forge/wifi-hub.md).
4. **Seat the Stick correctly.** The contacts seat magnetically — let it settle into position rather than forcing it.
5. **Give it a full hour.** A deeply drained Stick can take time before it shows progress in the app.

> **Brand-new Stick that seems dead?** That's expected. New Sticks ship in a low-voltage protection state. Leave it on a *powered* charger for about 10 seconds and the protection state clears — then it wakes when you lift it off.

If it still won't charge after cleaning, drying, and a full hour on a confirmed-powered charger, [fill out a ticket in the app](https://tms.fyi/help) and our Support Team will help.
