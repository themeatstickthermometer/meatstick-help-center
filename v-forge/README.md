# V Forge Set

The **V Forge Set** is built for the low-and-slow — smokers, kamados, pellet grills, overnight BBQ. It pairs a V Series Stick with a **Wired Ambient Probe** that reads pit temperature right at the grate, plus a **WiFi Hub** for cloud monitoring. Everything for your V Forge is here.
