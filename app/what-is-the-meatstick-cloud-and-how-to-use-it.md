# What is The MeatStick Cloud, and how to use it?

> **Got a V Series set?** Your Smart Base, Duo Base, or WiFi Hub connects to The MeatStick Cloud over WiFi on its own — you don't need the Bridge or a second device. See [Monitoring your cook over WiFi](../cooking-and-care/wifi-cloud-monitoring.md). The steps below are for Sticks without a WiFi-capable base.

**What is The MeatStick Cloud:**\
The MeatStick Cloud serves as a convenient platform that allows you to upload your cooking data to the cloud. This data includes information about your cooks and The MeatStick usage. By using an internet connection, your phone, tablet, or the Bridge can transfer this data to the cloud. Once uploaded, the data can be accessed and downloaded by other devices, such as phones or tablets, through the internet.

**How to Use The MeatStick Cloud:**\
To utilize The MeatStick Cloud, follow these steps:

* **Sign Up or Log In:** Create a free MeatStick account or log into your existing one on each phone or tablet that you wish to connect to The MeatStick Cloud.
* **Internet-Connected Device:** Ensure you have a phone or tablet with an active internet connection.
* **Bridge or Internet Connection:** You will need either the Bridge connected to Wi-Fi with internet access, or you can use a spare phone/tablet with an internet connection via Wi-Fi or mobile data.

**Cellular Data Usage:**

When your device is not connected to a Wi-Fi network with internet access, The MeatStick Cloud will use your cellular data. If you want to control your data usage, you can choose to disable The MeatStick Cloud in the app settings.

Feel free to enjoy the benefits of The MeatStick Cloud, enhancing your cooking experience through convenient data sharing.&#x20;
