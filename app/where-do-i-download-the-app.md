# Where do I download the App?

You can easily find and download "The MeatStick" app from the respective app stores. The app is published by Soma Labs LLC.

For _**iOS**_ Users:

Search for "The MeatStick" app on the App Store, or you can download it [here](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/themeatstick/id1353339890). The app is compatible with iOS 12 or newer.

For _**Android**_ Users:

For Android 12 or later, search for "The MeatStick" app on the Google Play Store,  or you can download it [here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.themeatstick.app).&#x20;

For Android 12 and older or using one of our older Red colored products, search for "The MeatStick Red" app on the Google Play Store,  or you can download it [here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.themeatstickred.app).&#x20;

Once you've downloaded the app, you'll be ready to explore its features and enhance your cooking journey.
