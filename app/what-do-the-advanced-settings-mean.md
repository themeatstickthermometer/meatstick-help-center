# What do the Advanced Settings mean?

The MeatStick App's Advanced Settings each serve a specific purpose:

**Demo Mode:** \
Enabling Demo Mode ensures that cooks created by an account are exclusively editable by the owner of that account. This mode is designed for demonstrations, allowing others to follow a cook on their own devices without modifying the cook settings.

**Stick Registration:** \
Activating Stick Registration limits your app to detecting only registered Sticks while ignoring others. This feature is handy in shared environments where multiple users employ The MeatStick, such as community kitchens, parks, or BBQ competitions.

**Stick Color Update:** \
This feature is utilized to correct the color of your Stick in the event of a reset. To initiate this function, contact our Support Team to obtain a permission code. You can submit a support request from the app by navigating to Menu -> Help -> Email (upper right corner) > Email Support. Select your desired Stick color and follow the in-app instructions.

**Developer Tools:** \
This feature is made especially for the MeatStick developer team

**Factory Restore:** \
This feature is utilized to restore a Stick’s setting to its factory setting, in the event of an error on the Stick. Please consult our Support Team first before utilizing this function in the case of an error. You can submit a support request from the app by navigating to Menu -> Help -> Email (upper right corner) > Email Support.&#x20;

These Advanced Settings offer additional control and customization for your MeatStick experience. We’re always adding new features in the future to better the MeatStick experience.
