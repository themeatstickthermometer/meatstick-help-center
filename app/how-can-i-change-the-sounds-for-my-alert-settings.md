# How can I change the sounds for my alert settings?

To change your alert settings and customize the alert sounds, follow these simple steps:

1. Open the app and access the menu by clicking the three stripes (hamburger) menu icon.
2. Select "Settings" from the menu options.
3. Choose "Alert Settings" to access the alert customization options.
4. In this section, you can adjust the Alert Duration and select the desired Alert Sound.

By following these steps, you can easily personalize your alert settings to suit your preferences.
