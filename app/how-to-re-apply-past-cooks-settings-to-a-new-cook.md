# How to re-apply past-cooks settings to a new cook?

You can easily re-apply the settings from a past cook to a new one. Follow these steps:

1. When your Stick is detected, click on it to access its settings.
2. Choose the "Past Cooks" option when selecting your cooking methods.
3. You'll be directed to a list of your past cooks.
4. Select the desired past cook from the list and re-apply its settings to your new cook.

By following these steps, you can quickly and conveniently apply your previous cooking settings to your new cook.
